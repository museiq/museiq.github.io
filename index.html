About Me

My name is Alex Rice-Reynolds and I'm a software developer. Officially, I write backend code for new and existing software applications. In reality i'm more of a creative enthusiast with a mission to solve real life problems for real people. Which is a fancy way of saying that I have an emphasis in ICTD and love fixing things.
